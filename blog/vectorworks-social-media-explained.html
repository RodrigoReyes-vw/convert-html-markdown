Follow @Vectorworks on Social Media!

Posted by Alex Altieri  |  3 min read time

If you haven't followed Vectorworks' social media platforms yet, there's no better time than now! We're doing our first giveaway and you won't want to miss it. There's a serious prize at stake, so stay tuned for more details. 👀

From pictures of beautiful design projects to helpful information on using Vectorworks software, our social media channels are sure to have something for everyone. Let them bring you inspiration, software tips, and so much more!

Instagram

Instagram is where we like to show off excellent designs! Customers who use #MadeWithVectorworks are considered for re-sharing on our account to bring recognition to a variety of designers and styles. Have your design featured by tagging @Vectorworks or by submitting to #MadeWithVectorworks here.

Our Instagram is carefully curated, so you'll see a steady stream of stunning designs. We also like to post to our story about everything that's happening at Vectorworks, so Instagram is a great place to drop a follow — especially to participate in the giveaway!

LinkedIn

LinkedIn is where we seek to help design professionals like you with learning opportunities such as webinars and informative blog posts to help you make the most of your Vectorworks experience and, in turn, bring your designs to their fullest potential.

A wide variety of tutorial-like content makes LinkedIn the place to go if you're looking for a consistent way to brush up on some Vectorworks skills or learn some new ones.

Twitter

Twitter is where we answer customer questions and participate in online conversations. We share much of our media mentions to show how we're making an impact in our industries. Just @ us if you're looking for a quick response to a question!

Facebook

Facebook is where we like to build a community with our users by sharing quick, helpful information that can aid your day-to-day Vectorworks experience.

 

Follow for company updates, video content, posts about our latest blogs, and more. One of the hallmarks of our Facebook page are weekly #TechTipTuesday videos, where we take a common user issue and show you how you can fix it in Vectorworks.

YouTube

YouTube is the ideal place to watch short tutorials on how to use Vectorworks software. Videos are created by product experts at Vectorworks and often center around common tasks or workflows you might encounter. 

 

With yearly releases of the next version of Vectorworks, we upload a series of video tutorials that break down new features and capabilities. It's a great place to subscribe to stay in tune with the newest Vectorworks features.
